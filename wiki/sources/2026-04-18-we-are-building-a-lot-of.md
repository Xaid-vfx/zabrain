# Free tool arbitrage as distribution funnel

**Summary:** Anas and his co-founders are shipping AI SaaS products fast but can't sell them — distribution is the bottleneck. His proposed fix: "free tool arbitrage" — clone paid SaaS tools, launch free alternatives to aggregate users, then use that audience as the top of a funnel for future paid products. Concrete first move: rebuild Cursorful (a $100 lifetime-access demo-video Chrome extension) for free in one night with Claude, launch it publicly, figure out the paid layer later.

**Key phrase:** "we are building sort of like a user acquisition funnel over here in which our top days customers who are our free customers are going to be our potential paid customers later on"
