# Data-driven growth sprint playbook

**Summary:** Anas lays out how every growth motion should start: reverse-engineer winning campaigns (not the surface creative, the psychology behind why they convert), use high-bid keywords to find demand, mine the Meta ad library and scraped cross-channel ad data to see who competitors target and what messaging works at scale. The recurring levers are scarcity, urgency, identity, and social proof — the same across every industry. Build an ICP off real conversion signals, design a predictive sprint before anything goes live, then once it's running track every dollar through every funnel step, touchpoint, conversion event, and endpoint.

**Key phrase:** "the data is right there — you just need to look for it"
