# Distribution & growth playbook

Anas is circling the same core problem from two angles: if you can build AI SaaS at lightning speed, distribution — not product — is the real bottleneck. One angle is a bootstrap move for founders with no audience and no budget: free-tool arbitrage to aggregate users, then convert them later. The other is the paid, data-driven motion you run once something is live: reverse-engineer winning campaigns, scrape ad data, build ICPs off real conversion signals, track every dollar through every touchpoint. Together they sketch a rough go-to-market stack — earn audience first, then run predictive sprints against it.
